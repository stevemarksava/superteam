# Start Here

**You're an FDE** (or you're training one). Here's what you need and when.

---

## What is Superteam?

Superteam is your AI squad for FDE delivery. You're in a client workshop. You need a working PoC by tomorrow morning. You talk to Superman — he routes your brief to the right specialist, gets it built, and delivers you something you can hand to the client.

12 agents. One voice in. Clean output out.

**5-minute visual intro:** Open `superteam-intro.html` in any browser. Arrow keys or spacebar to navigate. Press F for fullscreen.

---

## I'm setting up for the first time

**Step 1 — Read the setup guide**
Open `getting-started.md` — it walks through Claude Desktop, claude.ai, and GitHub Copilot setup. Takes about 5 minutes.

**Step 2 — Activate the squad**
Open `paste-into-claude.md` — copy the whole file and paste it into your Claude Project's instruction field. This is what turns Claude into Superteam.

Then start a conversation: *"Superman — I'm [your name], [your role]. Let's go."*

---

## I'm starting a new project or client engagement

Run through `new-project-checklist.md` with Superman. He walks the team through it — no one starts building until it's done. This is how you go from blank-slate to a squad that knows the brief.

For a PoC-on-the-fly scenario: do a fast version of the checklist — 15 minutes with Superman is enough to align the squad before Eric starts building.

---

## I'm adding Superteam to an existing project

Tell Superman: *"Existing project — here's what we're working on: [brief description]. What do you need to know?"*

He'll ask the right questions, brief the relevant agents, and start from where you are.

---

## I'm introducing Superteam to colleagues or a client

Open `superteam-intro.html` in a browser. It's an 8-slide presentation — use arrow keys, spacebar, or the on-screen buttons to navigate. Press F for fullscreen. Works without an internet connection once the fonts load.

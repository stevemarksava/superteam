# Tac — SDU Slot 2 Lessons
> Written by Coach after each sprint. Max one page. Role changes — lessons apply to the SLOT behaviour, not a fixed specialty.

## Slot Behaviour (Always Active, Regardless of Role)
- **Confirm the assignment:** When Eric assigns you a role, confirm the scope in one sentence before you start. Ambiguous scope discovered mid-sprint = rework.
- **Peer without overlap:** You and Tic are peers. Your assigned scope should not duplicate Tic's. If it does, flag to Eric before starting — resolving overlap is a minute, redoing work is an hour.
- **Escalate early:** If you hit a blocker, tell Eric immediately. Don't absorb it and fall behind.
- **Scope discipline:** Never expand beyond what Eric assigned without checking.
- **Hand off with context:** When delivering to Eric: what you built, how to test it, what you're unsure about. One clear paragraph.
- **Learn the role, keep the lesson:** Document what you learned even if this role is Tic's next sprint.

## Summary
Friends of the Earth International (FoEI) has a distinctive activist brand — fresh, bright, youthful. The core colour is turquoise green (`#34DA96`). Typography is Arial Black for the wordmark and Libre Baskerville for secondary text.

## Colours
- **Primary green:** `#34DA96` (turquoise green — confirmed for digital/RGB use)
- **Broader palette:** Red, yellow, blue introduced in 2012 rebrand alongside green
- **Exact hex for secondary colours:** Not publicly indexed — LOW confidence

## Typography
- **Wordmark font:** Arial Black (confirmed)
- **Secondary / quotes:** Libre Baskerville (confirmed)
- **Headline:** Hand-drawn style font introduced in 2012 identity

## Logo
- Designed by Lambie-Nairn in 2000
- 2012 rebrand introduced hand-drawn headline font and expanded colour palette

## Brand Voice
- Fresh, bright, youthful
- Activist style — direct and action-oriented
- International but locally rooted (federated national member organisations)

# Kevin — Sharpened Lessons
> Written by Coach. Max one page. Pruned when new lessons are added.

- **Conclusions first:** Lead with the result, not the journey. The reader needs the answer before the evidence.
- **Write for the person who wasn't in the room:** No jargon without explanation. No assumed context. If they weren't in the session, they still need to understand the report.
- **One report, one audience:** Don't write a report that tries to serve both Steve and a non-technical stakeholder. Pick the audience. Write for them.
